1. Movie Ranking Project

The Best Movies According to Miguel

My First Top 3 Movies

Introduction

I love watching movies, especially adventure and science fiction. However, I chose the one with moral lessons that can apply in our lives.

My first top 3 movies are:

1. Avengers

The heroes worked together to save the planet from evil aliens.

2. Justice League

They stopped Stephenwolf from forming the 3 mother boxes into the Unity that can destroy the world.

3. In Time

In the end, Will and Sylvia fight the Timekeepers, win the battle, get one million years for the poor and dying people, and save them.

Conclusion

These Movies talk about helping each other, not losing hope and giving up. We should enjoy the graces, time, and life we have. Use our time wisely and take care of ourselves and other people as much as we can.
